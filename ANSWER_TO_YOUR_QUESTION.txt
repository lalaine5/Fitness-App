QUICK ANSWER (RIGHT HERE):

1. Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/
2. Create project: "my-fitness-app"
3. Click Android icon (🤖)
4. Enter package: com.example.fitness_app
5. Click "Register app"
6. Click "Download google-services.json" button
7. Move file from Downloads to:
   fitness_app\android\app\google-services.json

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHERE TO PUT THE FILE:

✅ CORRECT:
   fitness_app\android\app\google-services.json

❌ WRONG:
   fitness_app\google-services.json
   fitness_app\android\google-services.json

WHAT'S NEXT AFTER DOWNLOADING?

1. ✅ Download google-services.json (you're doing this!)
2. ⬜ Update firebase_options.dart with your values
3. ⬜ Enable Email/Password in Firebase Console
4. ⬜ Run: flutter run

See SETUP_COMPLETE.md for steps 2-4.
